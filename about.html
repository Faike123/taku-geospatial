About Me

Hi, I'm Taku — a geoscientist and data enthusiast passionate about exploring Earth and beyond. My journey combines geography, technology, and a deep curiosity about landscapes, systems, and the unseen patterns beneath the surface.

With experience in remote sensing, GIS, planetary science, and geospatial data management, I strive to create tools, maps, and insights that drive meaningful understanding and action.

What Drives Me

  • Exploration: From Scottish glens to Martian craters, I love discovering new terrain — physical or digital.
  • Data for Impact: I believe in turning complex data into decisions that matter.
  • Interdisciplinary Thinking: I bring together science, design, and computation to solve real-world problems.
  • Learning & Sharing: Whether in the field or behind a screen, I’m always learning — and I love to teach and collaborate.
